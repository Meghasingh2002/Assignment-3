About Us

Born in 1946 as a small pastry shop in Alba, Italy, Ferrero is now present in more than 50 countries with 32 manufacturing plants across five continents. Our products are available in more than 170 countries. Today, Ferrero and its related companies consist of over 46,000 collaborators from more than 120 different nationalities.
